Our Projects & Work

Corporate Banking

Helping Indonesia MSME to enter the Global Field

Contextual Background

Our client is one of the largest Indonesian state-owned bank. As a part of their corporate banking services, our client supports MSME customers to enter the cross-border market through financial and business support. Furthermore, at the same time, to acquire new customer leads through this service.

The Challenge

Our client is looking to create a digital platform for its customer to access various financial services and business support.
AMI Consulting was commissioned to identify and understand the kind of technology that aligns with Indonesia’s MSME digital preparedness and desire to use through survey and in-depth interview.

The Approach

Measuring attractiveness of various form of financial platforms.

Profiling Indonesia’s MSME based on size, location, and industry.

Identifying pain points and needs of Indonesia’s MSME on each profile.

Projecting potential outcome of each new financial platform and target user.

Result

We successfully map out Indonesia’s MSME profile and digital preparedness.
With that data, we measure the potential revenue for each types of technology.
Through that insights and recommendation, our client opens up into a potential revenue up to Rp 265 bn.

Commerce

Winning the Indonesian Social Commerce Market

Contextual Background

Our client is a SaaS e-commerce startup originated from USA with operations in extending to southeast Asia.

The Challenge

Our client is looking to enter and win the Indonesian Market with its social commerce value proposition. AMI Consulting  was  commissioned  to  understand  the  social  commerce  competitive landscape  in Indonesia, understand customer profile and behavior, and synthesize a Go-to-Market strategy for our client to penetrate the Indonesian Market. AMI consulting also acts as a Project Management Officer for the initial stage of implementation in Indonesia.

The Approach

Identifying key players and social commerce competitive landscape in Indonesia.

Understanding social and shopping behavior for several customer demographics.

Consolidate insights into an executable Go-to-Market Strategy with a distinctive USP.

Manage GTM execution by building human resources and digital platform.

Result

We successfully created a Go-to-Market Strategy which we help to execute.
In a course of 3 month, we helped our client to onboard 2 business counterparts and created a social commerce platform based on gathered insights about Indonesia’s customer. Our people-centered strategy creates a distinctive unique-selling point for our client which ensures long-term sustainability.
